No. I

Why solar.

Three reasons people on our books in Halifax fitted solar last year. None of them were the climate — though that’s a fine reason too.

  1. 01

    Lower bills, fixed for 25 years.

    An average 4 kW system in Halifax saves around £900–£1,200 a year on electricity. Add a battery and that figure climbs further. Once installed, your sunlight is free until 2050.

  2. 02

    Independence from the grid.

    Energy prices doubled in three years. Generating your own electricity is the only way to step out of that game permanently. With a battery, you can sail through power cuts.

  3. 03

    Free fuel for your car.

    Pair solar with a diverter-capable EV charger and your car charges from sunlight. The combination pays back faster than either system alone.

No. III

How we work.

Four steps, in order. There are never any surprises in the middle.

  1. i.

    Survey

    We come to you, look at the roof, the loft, the consumer unit. We measure shading and check the structure. The visit is free and you're under no obligation.

  2. ii.

    Quote

    A clear, itemised quote, written in plain English. We will tell you, honestly, if solar isn't right for your roof. No pressure, ever.

  3. iii.

    Install

    Most domestic systems are commissioned in one to three days. We turn up when we say we will, dust-sheet your hallways, and put the kettle back where you left it.

  4. iv.

    Aftercare

    25-year panel warranty as standard. We answer the phone ourselves — not a call centre — and we're still here a decade later if you want to add a battery, an EV charger, or a second array.
