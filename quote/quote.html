Terms

Payment

The site is built. Full payment (£400, plus £180 hosting and the £195 CMS option if taken) is due in full before the site is published. Payment by bank transfer; details on the invoice.

Ownership

You own the design, code, copy and assets outright. Source code is zipped and emailed on the day payment clears. Re-host elsewhere any time, with no permission required.

Hosting & uptime

Target uptime is 99.9%, measured against the upstream provider's status page (about 8.7 hours per year of unscheduled downtime allowed). No service credits or refunds for downtime caused by Vercel, your DNS provider, or anything else outside my direct control.

Edits

£50 per edit, completed within 5 working days. Same-day urgent edits £100. Out-of-scope work (new pages, layout changes, new features) quoted separately at £75/hour, 1-hour minimum.

Renewal

Hosting renews annually. Invoice issued 30 days before renewal. 30 days' written notice required to cancel; the site stays live until the end of the paid period.

Handover & exit

You already hold the source code, so handover is trivial: I send the deployment configuration and the domain transfer code, and another developer can re-host immediately. No additional handover fee.

CMS scope

The CMS lets you edit existing content — copy, images, testimonials, project case studies. It is not for layout, structural or design changes; those remain quoted as bespoke work.
